Marvel TV

The Marvel Cinematic Universe has been on the small screen for many years, since the creation of Marvel Television in 2010. Many TV series following the adventures of several different Marvel heroes were produced over the years, appearing on ABC television, Netflix, Hulu, and Freeform.

In 2018, Marvel Studios took over production of all television series, initiating a new wave of adventures destined to be broadcast on Disney's proprietary network, Disney+. These series began appearing in 2021, and will continue on into a glorious future.
